About

I'm S. Adithya — a self-taught maker and entrepreneur who focuses on hardware-first products. I design embedded systems (STM32, ESP32), build agricultural drones for spraying and seeding, and run workshops that teach students hands-on robotics and IoT.

I believe great products come from rapid prototyping, iteration, and user testing — I enjoy building the full stack: firmware, electronics, mechanical fixtures, and simple web tools to control them.
